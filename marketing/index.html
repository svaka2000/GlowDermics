The science layer

Not another vibes app.

Behind the warm UI sits an analysis pipeline built around dermatology research — not influencer trends.

Sixteen-dimension skin model

Maps to the dimensions dermatologists actually use to describe skin: hydration, barrier function, photodamage indicators, pigmentation, follicular activity, dermal density, and more.

Regional findings

Every analysis returns up to twelve regional findings — cheek, forehead, T-zone, perioral, peri-orbital — so you know where something is happening, not just that something is.

Trajectory, not snapshots

One scan is a moment. Velumi compounds. We model your trend across weeks and seasons, surface what's actually moving, and flag changes that need attention.

Brand-agnostic recommendations

Vera doesn't sell you our cream. There is no Velumi cream. Recommendations point to the products your skin actually needs — at the retailer you actually use.
